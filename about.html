Janice Liu is a children's comic creator and the artist of the 2019 graphic novel Chicken Soup & Goji Berries 中药鸡汤.

When she's not drawing, Janice runs art classes at Young Artists' Place, her studio in Burnaby, BC. She also teaches workshops and art programs for children across the Vancouver area.
