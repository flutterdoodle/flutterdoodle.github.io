Janice Liu is a children's illustrator and comic artist. Her work is often about fantasy, and Asian history and culture; her current project is drawing Chicken Soup & Goji Berries 中药鸡汤, a magical slice-of-life webcomic about a Chinese-Canadian family. When she's not working or drawing, Janice teaches art classes for teens at her private studio in Vancouver, BC.
