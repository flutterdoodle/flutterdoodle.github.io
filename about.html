Janice Liu is a children's illustrator, comic creator, and educator. She is the artist of Chicken Soup & Goji Berries 中药鸡汤, and her comic shorts have appeared in Wayward Sisters comic anthology, New Moon Girls, and Augur magazine. Her work often combines the flavours of her two favourite things: fantasy and Asian history.

Janice lives in the Vancouver area. When she's not drawing, she teaches art classes at her private studio in Burnaby, BC.
