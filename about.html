Janice Liu is a children's illustrator and comic creator. She is the artist of Chicken Soup & Goji Berries 中药鸡汤, and her latest project is a short for Wayward Sisters, a comic anthology to be kickstarted by TO Comix Press. Her work often combines the flavours of her two favourite things: fantasy and Asian history.

When she's not working or drawing, Janice teaches art classes for teens at her private studio in Vancouver, BC.
