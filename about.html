Janice Liu is a self-taught children's illustrator and comic artist. Her work is often about fantasy, and Asian history and culture; currently she draws Chicken Soup & Goji Berries 中药鸡汤, a magical slice-of-life webcomic about a Chinese-Canadian family.

When she's not working or drawing, Janice teaches art classes for teens at her private studio in Vancouver, BC.
