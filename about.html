Janice Liu is a children's comic creator, illustrator, and teaching artist. Her work often combines the flavours of her two favourite things: fantasy and Asian history.

When she's not drawing, Janice teaches art classes at her private studio in Burnaby, BC. She also does workshops and art programs for children across the Vancouver area.
